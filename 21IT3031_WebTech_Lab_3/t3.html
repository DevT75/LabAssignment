Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

    List of Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Tomato
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Toast bread with butter in pan.
  2. Add flavours and spices to it.
  3. Cook bacon in pan with butter and tomato.
  4. Serve the sandwich with tomato fill and bacon and enjoy.
